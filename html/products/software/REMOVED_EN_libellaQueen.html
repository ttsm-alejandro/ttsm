Tokyo Boeki Techno-System Ltd is proud to provide exceptional metrology software for use in conjunction with surveying instruments.

Libella Queen


Libella Queen offers a comprehensive selection of functions in order to accommodate all requirements in any environment. Libella Queen is linked with an articulated three-dimensional measuring machine, making measurement work with easy operation possible. The software enables the visual inspection of product results on the spot.

Informed by Your Feedback


To fully support manufacturing processes, we’ve incorporated a wealth of genuine opinions from field technicians. This pursuit has led to increased functionality and ease of use, as well as support for customers’ measurement and inspection work. As a result, our tool has become a dependable standard within Japanese automaker processes.

Product Features

  • Real-time Coordinate Value Display Dialog
  • Drawing Display of Measurement Data
  • Real-time Measuring Device Probe Position Display
  • Message Display of Measurement Operation Procedure
  • Measurement Calculation Result Display
  • History Display

It’s not necessary to prepare CAD data as only measurements are required to model results. In addition to the alignment function, various positioning in various specified conditions is possible. It’s also possible to measure a variety of elements, including the hole, pin, cone, and more. Cross-section and scanning measurements are effortlessly performed thanks to the electronic guiding function.

Our software makes it possible to omit the middle icon operation using a simple macro function. This permits repeated measurements of the same procedure with ease.

Wizard functionality is supported by our software. Unlike the macro function that repeats a process, this functionality allows users to add processing such as tolerance judgement to the measurement procedure. The results of the contact measurement may be exported directly in Excel™ spreadsheet format. The report is completed without handwriting the measurement result.
